WHY DID WE MAKE THIS WEBSITE?

F R I E N S H I P

Posted by Marcelo Zapata on June 11, 2023

Friendships are invaluable treasures that enrich our lives and leave lasting impressions. In the fast-paced world of technology and programming, I had the privilege of meeting an exceptional individual named Brian during our time at Florida State University (FSU). Our bond grew stronger as we embarked on numerous programming projects together. Brian, hailing from China, brought a unique perspective and dedication that made our collaboration unforgettable. As he prepares to return to China to pursue his master's degree, I can't help but reflect on the remarkable journey we've shared and the impact he has made on my life.

Humble Beginings

We quickly became partners in crime when it came to programming projects. Late nights spent debugging code, brainstorming innovative solutions, and working tirelessly to meet project deadlines became the norm for us. Our synergy was evident, and we complemented each other's strengths and weaknesses perfectly. Brian's analytical skills and attention to detail often astounded me, while my creative thinking and problem-solving abilities added a unique dimension to our collaborative efforts.

A lasting impact: As Brian prepares to return to China to pursue his master's degree, I can't help but feel a mix of emotions. On one hand, I'm proud of his accomplishments and excited for the opportunities that await him. On the other hand, I'll miss the daily interactions and shared moments that have become an integral part of our friendship

A lasting Friendship

However, I am grateful for the profound impact Brian has had on my life. Our friendship has not only enhanced my technical skills but also taught me the importance of cultural exchange, adaptability, and perseverance. Brian's dedication and unwavering commitment to his studies and personal growth serve as a constant inspiration to me. I have no doubt that he will achieve great things in his future endeavors. - Brian Ho 2023

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
